Thoughts about The Shawshank Redemption

Today, or perhaps let’s call it yesterday, I watched the movie The Shawshank Redemption.

Well, I knew the beginnings, like I knew the ends clearly. I just heard of it, I just accidentally came across a cut of classical movies which contains a special scene of this film. I kept thinking about until I went back to the dorm.

After taking a shower, lazy as I was, I decided to find sth to watch, maybe because it scores high, always on the board, or maybe because everyone keeps recommanding it, so I am a little curious about whether it is so charming or not. Now, after watching it, I don’t think I find the answer.

All I know till now is that DO NOT watch a classical and artful movie with

bullet screen on. Maybe because of them, I lost my thoughts, the view about it is almost formed from what I remembered from those bullet screens. Their feelings just occupied the feelings of mine.

People have said they gained the strength and hope from the characters, that them came to know themselves, and them found the way, while I didn’t get that feeling.

Maybe one sentence truly touched me: “Salvation lies within”.

Or two: “Get busy living, or get busy dying”.

I still didn’t find my way, after thinking from one time to another, I thought, maybe they are right.

Redemption won’t be here just after a person watches a movie, the redemption is to be found, to be found by oneself, nor from the movie, nor from others, but himself.

Who can say everyone should have found the hope and the redemption?

Someone did, but someone failed.

I admit that, it’s a splendid and classical movie, no wonder so many strayed people come to rewatch it, review it, and finally find their own way in their life. Maybe I just haven’t find yet, WHAT I should do, maybe is just keeping waiting, keeping waiting, and full of hopes.
